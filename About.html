ABOUT SCOTT

Scott Bodin, known as "Pictheas" on the web, is a queer Norwegian artist with a focus on fantasy, fairytales and folklore. He brings out the childlike wonder in the olden traditional style using digital modern tools. Storytelling has been his big love since childhood and he is always looking to tell a story through books, comics and illustration.

Through the years, Scott has worked with a good number of private clients creating character designs and illustrative works. He graduated with a bachelor in Visual Communication summer of 2024, and is now looking for work!
